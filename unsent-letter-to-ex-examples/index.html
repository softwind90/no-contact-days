Unsent letter to ex

Write the letter without handing them your healing.

An unsent letter gives the feeling somewhere to go without restarting the conversation.

Short unsent letter example

I miss you today, but I am not going to turn that feeling into a message. I can miss you and still choose distance.

Closure letter example

I wanted the ending to feel clearer than it did. I may not get the conversation I imagined, so I am giving myself permission to stop waiting for it.

Angry unsent letter example

I am angry about what happened, and I am allowed to be. I do not need to send this anger to prove it was real.

When to write one

Write when you want to text, when a memory hits, when you saw something online, or when you are tempted to ask for closure again.
