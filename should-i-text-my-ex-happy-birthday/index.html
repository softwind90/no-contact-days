Birthday text

Should you text your ex happy birthday during no contact?

If the message is really a bid for reassurance, it may not be as harmless as it looks.

Ask what you want from the message

A birthday text can look polite on the surface, but the emotional reason matters. Are you expecting warmth, a conversation, proof they miss you, or a chance to restart?

Reasons to pause

  • You will feel crushed if they do not reply.
  • You want the message to reopen contact.
  • You have checked their profile repeatedly today.
  • You are using the birthday to bypass your boundary.

If you decide not to send it

Write the message privately and let the date pass. Use the SOS pause if the urge is strong, or make a small milestone card instead.

If contact is necessary

Keep it short and do not add emotional hooks. No memories, no questions, no "hope you are well" if you know you are hoping for more.

Birthday test

Would I send this if they never replied?

If no, it is probably not just a birthday message.
