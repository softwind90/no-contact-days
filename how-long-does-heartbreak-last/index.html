Heartbreak timeline

Healing is not a countdown, but patterns do change.

You may not wake up suddenly over it. More often, the waves get shorter, the triggers get clearer, and your own life gets louder again.

The first week

The first week can feel like withdrawal from a daily habit. Sleep, appetite, focus, and impulse control may all feel disrupted.

The first month

By a month, many people can identify patterns: late nights, weekends, social media, music, places, or unanswered questions.

After a few months

The relationship may still matter, but the pain may stop owning every hour. Progress often looks like longer stretches of calm, not constant happiness.

When to get more support

If you feel unsafe, unable to function, or stuck in intense distress, reach out to a licensed professional or local emergency resources.
