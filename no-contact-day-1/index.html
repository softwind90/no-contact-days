Day 1

No contact day 1 is about reducing damage.

You do not need a perfect healing plan today. You need a simple way to not make the wound louder.

What day 1 usually feels like

Day 1 can feel urgent, foggy, angry, hopeful, and empty at the same time. Your brain may look for one more message because a reply feels like relief. That relief usually does not last.

Your first 24-hour plan

  • Move the conversation thread out of sight.
  • Write the message in an unsent letter instead.
  • Eat something simple and drink water.
  • Tell one safe person that today is day 1.
  • Start a visible counter.

Do not negotiate with the urge

When the urge arrives, do not ask whether you should text forever. Ask whether you can wait ten minutes. Open the texting urge tool and put the sentence there first.
