Privacy

Your tracker entries stay in your browser.

No Contact Days does not require an account. Core tracker entries are saved to local browser storage on the device where you enter them.

Effective June 23, 2026No account required

Information you enter into the tools

Names or nicknames, dates, check-ins, urges, notes, unsent letters, and memory-vault entries are stored in your browser using local storage. The site does not provide account-based cloud sync for this information.

How to remove local data

Use the tracker’s reset control or clear site data in your browser settings. Clearing browser storage, using a private browsing session, or moving to another device can remove or hide locally saved entries.

Basic site events

The site may keep a small local history of button and tool events in your browser to support product testing. This local event history is limited and is not an account profile. If third-party analytics is enabled in the future, this page will be updated to identify it.

Hosting and network data

Like most websites, the hosting and network providers may process standard request information such as IP address, browser type, requested URL, and timestamp to deliver, secure, and operate the site. Their handling of this data is governed by their own policies.

Cookies

The current core tools do not require advertising cookies or an account cookie. Browser storage is used for tracker functionality. If advertising, login, or additional analytics is introduced, this policy will be updated before those features are treated as part of the site.

Sensitive information

Do not enter passwords, payment information, legal documents, health records, precise addresses, or information that could put you or another person at risk. The tools are intended for short personal notes and dates, not secure document storage.

Changes to this page

This page will be revised when the site begins using a new data processor or changes how tool data is stored. The effective date at the top will change when the policy changes substantially.
