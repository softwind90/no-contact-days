Breakup days calculator

How many days has it been since your breakup?

Use this free calculator to count the days since the breakup and turn the number into a recovery milestone.

Why this number matters

The number is not a score. It is a marker. It helps you notice that time is moving, even when your emotions feel stuck.

What to do with your result

If the number is small, focus on getting through the next hour without reopening the wound. If you are near day 7 or day 30, treat that as a milestone worth protecting.

Next steps

  • Start a no-contact streak
  • Write an unsent letter instead of texting
  • Save a milestone card when you reach day 7 or day 30
