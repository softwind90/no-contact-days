No contact day 7

The first week is a real milestone.

Day 7 often feels harder than expected because the shock is fading and the habit of reaching out is still alive.

Why day 7 can feel hard

The first few days can run on adrenaline. Around one week in, the silence becomes more real. You may want reassurance, closure, or one more conversation.

What to do today

  • Do not check their social media for one full day.
  • Write the message in an unsent letter instead.
  • Save day 7 as a milestone, not a test you have to pass perfectly.

What day 7 means

It means you have already made it through multiple waves. That is evidence you can make it through the next one.
