No contact day 30

After a month, start looking for patterns.

Day 30 does not mean everything is fixed. It means you have enough distance to notice what still pulls you back and what has started to soften.

What may change by day 30

The urge to text may still appear, but it may not last as long. Certain triggers become clearer: weekends, late nights, mutual friends, songs, places, or social media.

What to review

  • Which days had the strongest urges?
  • Which triggers repeated?
  • What helped you not reopen contact?
  • What do you want the next 30 days to protect?

Do not use day 30 as an excuse to text

A milestone is proof that distance helps. It is not automatically a sign that you are ready to restart contact.
