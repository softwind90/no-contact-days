Stop checking your ex

Social media checking keeps the breakup open.

You may not be texting them, but every profile check can restart the waiting, comparing, and guessing.

Why checking hurts

Social media gives fragments without context. A story, like, follow, or photo can turn into hours of interpretation.

Make checking harder

  • Mute or block temporarily if you need distance.
  • Remove shortcuts and search history.
  • Use app limits during late-night hours.
  • Ask a friend to hold you accountable for the first week.

Replace the action

When your hand moves toward the app, open your tracker, write one unsent sentence, or log the trigger. The goal is not perfect discipline. It is a different next move.
