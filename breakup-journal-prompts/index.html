Journal prompts

Breakup journal prompts for the days you want to text.

Use a prompt to move the feeling somewhere safer before you reopen a conversation that may hurt you.

Prompts for the urge to text

  • I want to text because I am hoping for...
  • If they do not reply, I will probably feel...
  • The need under this message is...
  • A safer way to meet that need tonight is...

Prompts for missing them

  • What exactly do I miss: the person, the routine, or the version of myself I had with them?
  • What part of the relationship hurt more than I admitted?
  • What memory can I keep without using it as a reason to go back?

Prompts for clarity

  • What pattern do I not want to repeat?
  • What did this breakup teach me about my boundaries?
  • What would a calmer version of me choose for the next 24 hours?

If you want a place to store these privately, use the tracker or the unsent letter examples.
