Why tools help during no contact

No contact is hardest when the next action is unclear. A tool gives the feeling somewhere to go: count the days, write the unsent message, log the urge, or turn a milestone into something visible.

If you are not sure where to start, use the no contact calculator first. If the number makes you want to reach out, open the texting urge pause before sending anything.
